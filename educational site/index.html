Did You Know?


Physics is an incredibly fascinating subject that helps us understand the world around us, from the smallest particles to the vast expanse of the universe. It’s not just about complex formulas and theories — it’s about the wonders and mysteries of nature that often surprise us in the most unexpected ways. Did you know that the theory of gravity was inspired by something as simple as an apple falling from a tree? Or that a day on Venus is longer than a year? These are just a few examples of the countless surprising facts that physics offers. Whether it’s the mind-boggling speed of light, the strange behavior of subatomic particles, or the quirks of space-time itself, physics never stops intriguing us. Let’s dive into some of these fascinating facts and uncover the secrets of the universe!
